Overview

We are looking for an experienced Lead Data Scientist to drive advanced analytics initiatives, with a strong focus on Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM).
This role combines leadership, business understanding, and technical depth, acting as a key bridge between business stakeholders and data teams, while ensuring high-quality delivery and alignment across the organization.


Key Responsibilities

Lead and coordinate a team of ~4 Data Scientists, ensuring alignment, productivity, and delivery quality
Align expectations with business stakeholders and translate needs into actionable data science initiatives
Act as the main point of contact between the data science team and the business
Collaborate closely with the Product Owner (PO) to ensure roadmap execution
Coordinate with other data teams to ensure consistency and integration across initiatives
Design, oversee, and validate Marketing Mix Models (MMM) and other advanced analytical solutions
Ensure models are interpretable, scalable, and aligned with business impact
Stay close to the technical work: review code, guide implementation, and step in when needed (without being fully hands-on)


Required Skills & Experience

10+ years of experience in Data Science or Advanced Analytics
Proven experience in Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)
Strong understanding of both mathematical modeling and business context
Experience leading and mentoring teams (3–5 people)


Strong experience with:
Python
Pyomo (or similar optimization frameworks)
Gurobi (or other optimization solvers)
GitHub


Ability to understand and review code while maintaining a leadership role
Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
Experience working in cross-functional and distributed environments


Nice to Have

Experience in large-scale marketing or commercial analytics environments
Background in optimization or econometrics
Experience working with multiple data teams in complex organizations


Contract Details

Duration: Initial 6 months (with potential for further extension)
Start date: ASAP (critical urgency)
Location: Flexible / remote


Ideal Profile

A senior profile who can lead, structure, and connect—someone who understands the math, speaks the language of the business, and can guide a team while staying close enough to the technical side to challenge and support effectively.
