Data Scientist

About the job
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist / AI Engineer to design and deliver end-to-end Machine Learning and AI solutions, from data analysis to model deployment.
This role follows a hybrid model based in Lisbon, and we welcome candidates open to relocating to Lisbon.


Key Responsibilities

Design, develop, and deploy ML/AI solutions (data preparation, modeling, evaluation).
Work closely with stakeholders to identify use cases and translate them into technical solutions and roadmaps.
Build and optimize data pipelines (ETL) using SQL and Python.
Support deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of models in production.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams and promote data-driven decision making.
Track and report project progress using tools like Jira and Confluence.
Requirements

6+ years of experience in Data, Analytics, or AI roles.
Strong knowledge of Machine Learning, statistics, and NLP techniques.
Proficiency in Python (or similar languages) and SQL.
Experience with ML frameworks (e.g. scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch).
Familiarity with CI/CD tools (Git, Jenkins).
Strong communication and stakeholder management skills.
Fluent English (C1+).
Additional Information

📍 Location: Lisbon
🏢 Work model: Hybrid (50%)
🌍 Open to candidates willing to relocate to Lisbon
