Data Scientist (Remote)

About the job
About Joveo:

Every company says they're "AI-first." We actually are. Joveo's recruitment 

advertising platform processes millions of hiring decisions through machine 

learning, real-time bidding, and predictive analytics — helping the world's 

largest employers find the right people, faster and fairer. But we're not 

done. Not even close.


Role: Data Scientist
Location: Remote


Role Overview:

We are hiring a Data Scientist to extract meaningful insights from Joveo's 

vast datasets and build predictive models that directly improve hiring 

outcomes for employers globally. You will work at the intersection of 

statistics, machine learning, and product — turning data into decisions.


Key Responsibilities:

- Design and build predictive models and statistical analyses to solve business problems
- Conduct exploratory data analysis on large, complex datasets to surface actionable insights
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to deploy models into production
- Define and monitor key metrics, run A/B experiments, and measure impact
- Communicate findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Maintain data science tooling, notebooks, and reproducible research pipelines


Required Skills & Qualifications:

- Strong proficiency in Python and the data science stack (pandas, scikit-learn, NumPy)
- Solid foundation in statistics, probability, and experimental design
- Experience with machine learning model development and evaluation
- Proficiency with SQL and large-scale data querying (BigQuery, Redshift, or Snowflake)
- Experience designing and analyzing A/B tests and causal inference studies
- Ability to present complex analysis clearly to non-technical audiences
