AI Engineer

About the job
We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer to join a dynamic and innovative team, working on cutting-edge artificial intelligence projects from proof of concept to production-ready solutions. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams and play a key role in shaping AI-driven products that solve complex business challenges.


📍 Work Model:
Hybrid Model (1 to 2 days/week at the client's location, in a region near Lisbon)


⏳ Minimum Experience
At least 5 years of professional experience as an AI Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, or in a similar role


✅ Key Requirements
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field
Proven experience developing and implementing AI and machine learning solutions
Strong knowledge of Large Language Models (LLMs) such as GPT
Solid understanding of machine learning and deep learning techniques and algorithms
Hands-on experience with frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras and/or LangChain
Proficiency in Python and Java
Experience with data manipulation, analysis and visualization libraries (NumPy, pandas, Matplotlib)
Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure and/or Google Cloud)
Strong problem-solving skills and ability to translate business requirements into technical solutions
Excellent communication and collaboration skills
Fluent English (spoken and written)
Advanced Portuguese (spoken and written)
Currently living in Portugal for at least 6 months


🧠 Responsibilities
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business needs and opportunities for AI-driven solutions
Design and develop proofs of concept (PoCs) using machine learning and deep learning techniques
Evaluate PoC performance in terms of accuracy, scalability and computational efficiency
Gather stakeholder feedback and iterate on PoCs to maximise business value
Analyse results and identify the most promising AI solutions
Translate successful PoCs into minimum viable products (MVPs)
Optimise and fine-tune AI models to meet performance and business requirements
Conduct thorough testing and validation to ensure reliability and usability
Support the integration of AI capabilities into existing systems or new AI-driven applications
Stay up to date with industry trends, best practices and emerging AI technologies
Document processes, methodologies and results clearly for technical and non-technical stakeholders
Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior AI engineers

If you thrive in fast-paced environments, enjoy working with LLMs, machine learning and deep learning, and are driven by continuous learning and innovation, this opportunity is for you.
