Junior Data Engineer (AWS)

About the job
We are looking for a Junior Data Engineer with around 1 year of experience to support the development and maintenance of data pipelines and cloud-based data solutions. This role is ideal for someone proactive, dedicated, and eager to grow in a fast-paced data environment.


Responsibilities
Develop and maintain data pipelines using Python
Support ETL/ELT processes and data ingestion from multiple sources
Work with AWS services such as S3, Lambda, and Glue
Ensure data quality, reliability, and proper documentation
Collaborate with data engineers, analysts, and other stakeholders


Requirements
~1 year of experience in data engineering or a similar role
Strong knowledge of Python and SQL
Experience with Cloud
Familiarity with Git and data pipeline concepts


Soft Skills
Proactive and self-driven
Dedicated and responsible
Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
Good communication and teamwork skills
Willingness to learn and continuously improve
